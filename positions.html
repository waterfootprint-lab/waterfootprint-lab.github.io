The Lab

Water Footprint Lab

We map freshwater use across the planet — and what it means for water security, ecosystems, and people. The lab is led by Dr. Mesfin Mekonnen at The University of Alabama's Department of Civil, Construction and Environmental Engineering, and focuses on global water footprint accounting, water scarcity assessment, agricultural water productivity, and the food–energy–water nexus.

We are always looking for motivated, quantitatively minded researchers who want to tackle real-world water challenges at scale. Strong candidates typically have a background in civil or environmental engineering, hydrology, GIS, remote sensing, or a related field.
