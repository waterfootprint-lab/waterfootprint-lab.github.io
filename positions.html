Ph.D. Research Assistantship

● No Open Positions available
Fully funded · Ph.D. · The University of Alabama · Rolling review

The Water Footprint Lab is recruiting motivated Ph.D. students to work on water footprint assessment, water scarcity analysis, agricultural water productivity, hydrologic modeling, or remote sensing of water use. Funding covers full tuition and a competitive graduate stipend.

We are looking for

  • B.S. or M.S. in civil/environmental engineering, hydrology, geography, or related field
  • Strong quantitative and analytical skills
  • Water security and sustainability expertise is a plus
  • Experience with programming (Python, R, MATLAB) and/or GIS/remote sensing
  • Motivation to publish and present research

We offer

  • Full tuition waiver + competitive stipend
  • Access to high-performance computing resources
  • Opportunities to collaborate with national/international partners
  • Support for conference travel and publications
